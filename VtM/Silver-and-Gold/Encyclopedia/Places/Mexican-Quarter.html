Buena Vista is one of the four semi-autonomous quarters within the San Francisco Free City. As such, it serves as an extraterritorial extension of Mexico.

Buena Vista has a population of about 200,000 people and is run by a democratically-elected Alcalde. 

Buena Vista has some of the sunniest weather in San Francisco, and has a large number of farms, the oldest of which (Blaylock Farms) dating back to the 1850s.
