Nature and Demeanor: Every character has a true nature, which describes them as they actually are, and a demeanor, which is the face they present to the world. One's nature may be the same as one's demeanor, similar, or completely different. Keeping one's nature is normally a secret from all but one's most trusted confidants (and maybe not even them); consider that if others know your true nature (and in game sense, how you regain Willpower), they have information that could be used against you.

Over time, one's Demeanor can change; however, Nature almost never does.

It is important to remember both Nature and Demeanor; remember that if you act according to your Nature, you may regain Willpower points; always bring such acts to the Storyteller's attention, secretly or otherwise. Roleplaying your Demeanor can net you bonus Experience Points.
